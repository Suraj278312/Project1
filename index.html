About Subway

Experience the finest gourmet sandwiches in a cozy and rustic atmosphere. At Subway, we are dedicated to delivering exceptional flavors that will tantalize your taste buds.

Our sandwich artists meticulously craft each sub using locally sourced ingredients and unique flavor combinations. From tender meats to mouthwatering vegetarian options, there's something for everyone at Subway.

Our Menu

Black Forest Ham

Black Forest Ham is a flavorful and slightly sweet ham, traditionally seasoned and smoked, often used in sandwiches and deli meats.

Spicy Pepperoni

Spicy Pepperoni is a zesty sausage made from pork and beef, seasoned with paprika and chili powder for a distinct heat.

Crispy Bacon

Crispy Bacon is a savory and crunchy strip of cured pork, perfect for adding a delicious crunch to sandwiches and breakfast dishes.
